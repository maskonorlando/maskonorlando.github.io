Our Products

Currently, we are 3D printing masks and touch tools.

Our products consist of 3D printed masks and touch tools. Our masks provide a full face covering and can be adjusted to various sizes. Our touch tools are compact, durable and promote sanitary measures. The material used is PLA which is plant-derived and biodegradable.

We are currently using an Ender 3 Pro to print out PPEs, however if you have a 3D printer at home you can print out your own mask or touch tool.
