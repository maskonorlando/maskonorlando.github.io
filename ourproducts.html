Our Products

Currently, we are 3D printing masks and touch tools.

Mask On Orlando is an organization focused to promote social distancing measures in the Central Florida region. Our main initiative is to distribute 3D printed masks and other personal protective equipment to those in need. We also plan to share creative techniques of making DIY masks and clarifying misinformation surrounding the COVID-19 pandemic. Please help us raise awareness by donating to support our cost of materials or sharing this link to our community.
